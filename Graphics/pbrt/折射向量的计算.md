条件如上（均为单位向量）。

$$
\begin{aligned}
\eta_i \sin\theta_i &= \eta_t \sin\theta_t \\
|i_{\parallel}| &= |i| \sin\theta_i \\
|t_{\parallel}| &= |t| \sin\theta_t \\
|t_{\parallel}| &= \frac{\eta_i}{\eta_t}|i_{\parallel}| \\
t_{\parallel} &= \frac{\eta_i}{\eta_t}i_{\parallel}
\end{aligned}
	$$
